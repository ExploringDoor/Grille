Our Story

Shore Food Done Right

Fishin' Pier Grille has been a Jersey Shore staple since 2006, serving up fresh, made-to-order breakfast and lunch to locals and vacationers alike. What started as a small kitchen with a big heart has grown into two beloved locations across Stone Harbor and Avalon.

Every dish is prepared with care, using quality ingredients and recipes perfected over nearly two decades of early mornings and busy summers. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite before hitting the beach or settling in for a long brunch with family, we're here to make your morning right.
